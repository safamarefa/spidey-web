Spider-Man

Spider-Man is a fictional superhero from Marvel Comics, created by Stan Lee and Steve Ditko. He first appeared in Amazing Fantasy #15 in 1962. As an orphan raised by Aunt May and Uncle Ben, Spider-Man faces both everyday challenges and his role as a hero. His superpowers include strength, speed, agility, and a "spider-sense" that alerts him to danger. He also uses "web-shooters," his invention that allows him to shoot webs.

Gwen Stacy

Gwen Stacy, Spider-Man’s first love, is a bright and compassionate college student known for her iconic blonde hair and classic style. Her tragic death at the hands of the Green Goblin deeply impacts Peter Parker, marking a pivotal moment in Spider-Man’s story. In alternate universes, she gains spider powers as "Spider-Gwen," reimagining her as a hero in her own right.
